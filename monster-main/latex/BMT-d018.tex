\par{Given a \ForesterRef{BMT-d001}{language}, \(\mathcal {L}\), a tuple of variables, \(x\), and \ForesterRef{BMT-d009}{\(\mathcal {L}\)-formulas}, \(\varphi (x)\) and \(\varpsi (x)\), \(\varphi (x)\) and \(\varpsi (x)\) are \emph{\(T\)-equivalent} if \(T  \models   \forall  x ( \varphi (x)  \leftrightarrow   \varpsi (x))\). }\par{It is simple to show that \(T\)-equivalence of formulas is an equivalence relation.}
