\par{Let \(I\) be any set. Then an ultrafilter \(\mathcal {U}\) on \(\mathcal {P}(I)\) is a proper \ForesterRef{BMT-d071}{filter} on \(I\) with the additional property:}\par{For every \(A \subseteq  I\), either \(A \in \mathcal {U}\) or \(I \backslash  A \in \mathcal {U}\).}\par{Every proper filter can be extended into an ultrafilter.}
